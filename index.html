Hotshotz Bar and Grill

Welcome to Hotshotz Bar and Grill! If you are looking for cold drinks, delicious food, and great times in Smyrna, TN look no further than Hotshotz!! Come play pool or darts, sing karaoke, show off your dance moves or stop in for a drink on bike night!

We are located at 1208 South Lowry Street, Smyrna, TN. Come by and see us!
